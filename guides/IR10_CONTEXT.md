# IR10 Financial Statements Summary – Field Guide

> **Purpose**: Reference for completing the NZ IRD IR10 form. All figures are financial accounting figures (GST-exclusive unless accounts are GST-inclusive), except Boxes 28, 29, 52, and 59 which are tax figures.

---

## Box 1 – Multiple Activity Indicator
- Tick **Yes** only if the business has more than one distinct line of business (e.g., window cleaning AND painting contractor).
- Tick **No** for multiple divisions of the same business or multiple locations of the same business type.

---

## PROFIT & LOSS – INCOME (Boxes 2–11)

### Box 2 – Sales and/or Services
Gross income from sale of goods/services (turnover). Include management fees/commissions if main income source. Include gross salary/wages/schedular payments if only income; otherwise put those in Box 10.

### Box 3 – Opening Stock
Total stock at start of year: raw materials, livestock, grain/produce, WIP, finished goods. Should match last year's closing stock.

### Box 4 – Purchases
Total purchases + other direct costs from P&L. If only COGS shown: `Purchases = COGS + Closing Stock − Opening Stock`. Direct labour costs for service businesses go here. Exclude asset purchases reported in Box 26.

### Box 5 – Closing Stock
Total stock at end of year (same categories as Box 3). If turnover ≤ $1.3M and estimated closing stock < $10,000, opening stock may be used (s EB 23 ITA 2007).

### Box 6 – Gross Profit
`= Box 2 − (Box 3 + Box 4 − Box 5)` i.e., Sales minus Cost of Goods Sold, where COGS = Opening Stock + Purchases − Closing Stock. Equivalently: `Box 2 − Box 3 − Box 4 + Box 5`. If stock figures unavailable, copy Box 2 here. Losses: minus sign.

### Box 7 – Interest Received
Interest/finance income from P&L. Include finance lease interest income.

### Box 8 – Dividends Received
Dividends from P&L including inter-group. Can be grossed up for imputation credits or reported net with tax adjustment in Box 28. GAAP/IFRS entities must use net basis.

### Box 9 – Rental, Lease and Licence Income
Gross rental/lease/licence/hire income before expenses. Includes land, buildings, vehicles, fishing quotas, stud fees, franchise fees, royalties. If this is normal business activity, include in Box 2 instead. Finance lease interest → Box 7.

### Box 10 – Other Income
All other P&L income not in Boxes 2, 6–9. Includes: taxable property income, salary/wages/schedular payments not in Box 2, depreciation recovered, bad debts recovered, insurance receipts, gain on sale of assets (unless exceptional), goods for private use, wage subsidies. Exclude exceptional items (→ Box 26).

### Box 11 – Total Income
`= Box 6 + Box 7 + Box 8 + Box 9 + Box 10`. Should equal total P&L income excluding exceptional items. Losses: minus sign.

---

## PROFIT & LOSS – EXPENSES (Boxes 12–25)

> **Important**: If an expense category below was already included as a direct cost in COGS (and thus in Gross Profit at Box 6), do NOT include it again.

### Box 12 – Bad Debts
Bad debts written off. Exclude doubtful debts and debt collection fees.

### Box 13 – Accounting Depreciation and Amortisation
Total accounting depreciation + amortisation from P&L. If tax rates used for accounting, this equals Box 52.

### Box 14 – Insurance (exclude ACC levies)
Insurance premiums expensed. ACC levies go in Box 22.

### Box 15 – Interest Expense
Total interest/finance expense from P&L.

### Box 16 – Professional and Consulting Fees
Accounting, legal, tax advice, management advice, financial advisory, feasibility studies, M&A advice. Only expensed amounts (not capitalised).

### Box 17 – Rates
Rates paid to local/regional authorities (council rates, water rates).

### Box 18 – Rental, Lease and Licence Payments
Rental/hire/lease/licence payments for buildings, vehicles, equipment, franchise fees, royalties, licence fees. Do NOT net against Box 9 income.

### Box 19 – Repairs and Maintenance
All repairs and maintenance expenditure from financial statements.

### Box 20 – Research and Development
R&D expenditure expensed (not capitalised). Includes scientific, technological, industrial R&D, software development.

### Box 21 – Associated Persons' Remuneration
Remuneration to business owners (incl. sole traders) and associated persons (as defined in subpart YB, ITA 2007 – see IR620). Includes salaries, wages, lump sums, management fees, directors' fees to associated persons. May exclude indirect costs (ACC, FBT, KiwiSaver) for simplicity – put those in Box 24 instead. Does NOT include dividends, drawings, interest, or royalties.

### Box 22 – Salaries and Wages Paid to Employees
Salaries, wages, PAYE, bonuses, KiwiSaver contributions, ACC levies, other indirect employment costs. Exclude associated persons' remuneration (→ Box 21).

### Box 23 – Contractor and Sub-contractor Payments
Contractor/subcontractor payments: agricultural, construction, schedular payments, relief drivers, temps, labour-only contractors, IT contractors. Exclude associated persons' remuneration and employee wages.

### Box 24 – Other Expenses
All other P&L expenses not in Boxes 4, 12–23. Examples: admin costs, cleaning, communication, consumables, electricity, entertainment, travel, training, vehicle expenses, indirect remuneration from associated persons.

### Box 25 – Total Expenses
`= Sum of Boxes 12 to 24`.

---

## Box 26 – Exceptional Items
Large, non-recurring income/expense items. Only disclose if total > 5% of turnover. Categories:
1. Sale/disposal of business or significant part
2. Natural disasters
3. Major restructuring costs
4. Major impairments/write-offs
5. Reversal of major impairments/write-offs/restructuring provisions
6. Large one-off non-operational receipts

Negative amount: minus sign.

## Box 27 – Net Profit/Loss Before Tax
`= Box 11 − Box 25 ± Box 26`. Should match P&L net profit/loss before tax. Losses: minus sign.

## Box 28 – Tax Adjustments
All adjustments from accounting profit (Box 27) to taxable profit (Box 29). Can be positive or negative. Common causes:
- Accounting vs tax depreciation difference
- Investment Boost claimed / change in use
- Tax depreciation recovered
- Capital gains/losses on asset sales
- Provisions not incurred at balance date
- Tax-exempt inter-company dividends
- Non-deductible expenditure (e.g., entertainment)
- Imputation credits on dividends received
- Fair dividend rate income
- Income not in P&L (sole trader interest/dividends)
- Wages not paid within 63 days of balance date

Leave blank if Box 27 = Box 29.

## Box 29 – Current Year Taxable Profit/Loss
`= Box 27 ± Box 28`. Should match the tax return figure (before loss offsets/carryforwards):
- IR3 → Box 30
- IR3NR → Box 30
- IR4 → Box 26
- IR6 → Box 19B − Box 21
- IR7 → Box 24 (or add Box 28 less Box 18F)
- IR8 → Box 10J
- IR9 → Box 17

Losses: minus sign.

## BALANCE SHEET – ASSETS (Boxes 30–43)

### Current Assets
| Box | Field | What to include |
|-----|-------|-----------------|
| 30 | Accounts Receivable | All debtors/receivables at period end |
| 31 | Cash and Deposits | All bank accounts in funds, short-term deposits (<1yr), cash on hand, building society/stock firm funds |
| 32 | Other Current Assets | Closing stock/inventory, WIP, prepaid expenses, owners' current account (where owner owes business), GST refund owing |

### Fixed Assets (accounting book value, net of depreciation)
| Box | Field | What to include |
|-----|-------|-----------------|
| 33 | Vehicles | Cars, trucks, boats, trailers, motorbikes, tractors, aircraft, helicopters, caravans, forklifts, quad bikes |
| 34 | Plant and Machinery | Plant, machinery, loose tools (not vehicles) |
| 35 | Furniture and Fittings | Furniture, fittings, chattels, office equipment |
| 36 | Land | Land at cost or revalued amount |
| 37 | Buildings | Houses, factories, offices, barns, car parks (net of depreciation) |
| 38 | Other Fixed Assets | Any fixed assets not in 33–37 (fences, forests, orchards, vineyards, roads/tracks) |

### Other Non-Current Assets
| Box | Field | What to include |
|-----|-------|-----------------|
| 39 | Intangibles | Goodwill, software, patents, trademarks, RMA consents (net of amortisation) |
| 40 | Shares/Ownership Interests | Shares, partnership/JV interests, unit trusts, trust entitlements |
| 41 | Term Deposits | Long-term deposits (≥1yr), bonds. Not loans/mortgages |
| 42 | Other Non-Current Assets | Anything not in 30–41 (bonus bonds, deferred tax asset, loans receivable, mortgages receivable) |

### Box 43 – Total Assets
`= Sum of Boxes 30 to 42`. Must equal balance sheet total assets.

---

## BALANCE SHEET – LIABILITIES (Boxes 44–50)

### Current Liabilities
| Box | Field | What to include |
|-----|-------|-----------------|
| 44 | Provisions | Restructuring, redundancy, bonus provisions |
| 45 | Accounts Payable | Trade creditors, accruals at balance date |
| 46 | Current Loans | Loans repayable within 1yr, interest-free loans, owner loans, bank overdrafts, stock firm amounts |
| 47 | Other Current Liabilities | Any current liabilities not in 44–46, owners' current account (where business owes owner) |

### Box 48 – Total Current Liabilities
`= Sum of Boxes 44 to 47`.

### Box 49 – Non-Current Liabilities
Liabilities >1yr: loans, mortgages, term borrowing. Exclude owners' current account.

### Box 50 – Total Liabilities
`= Box 48 + Box 49`.

---

## Box 51 – Owners' Equity
`= Box 43 − Box 50`. Components by entity type:
- **Company**: paid-up capital + reserves + retained profits/accumulated losses
- **Partnership/Sole trader**: capital contributed + retained profits/accumulated losses
- **Club/Society**: accumulated funds + reserves
- **Trust/Estate**: amounts settled + undistributed profits/accumulated losses

Usually positive. Negative if accumulated losses exceed capital.

---

## OTHER INFORMATION (Boxes 52–60)

| Box | Field | Description |
|-----|-------|-------------|
| 52 | Tax Depreciation | Per ITA 2007 + depreciation determinations. Includes fixed assets, depreciable land improvements (sch 13), intangible property (sch 14), primary industry improvements (sch 20). Excludes buildings with useful life ≥50yrs. Also include Investment Boost claimed. |
| 53 | Untaxed Realised Gains/Receipts | Non-taxable gains: capital gains on land/shares/trademarks/goodwill, gifts, one-off capital receipts |
| 54 | Additions to Fixed Assets | Total spent on purchasing + commissioning fixed assets during year |
| 55 | Disposals of Fixed Assets | Proceeds from sale or scrapping of fixed assets |
| 56 | Dividends Paid | Dividends paid/credited to shareholder current accounts during year (incl. RWT, non-cash dividends). Exclude imputation credits and proposed-but-unpaid dividends |
| 57 | Drawings | Total drawings by proprietors/shareholders/partners/beneficiaries. Include private use adjustments and private expenditure not treated as dividends/distributions |
| 58 | Current Account Year-End Balances | Closing balances of all proprietor/shareholder/partner/beneficiary current accounts. Overdrawn = plus sign (+). Not overdrawn = minus sign (−) |
| 59 | Tax-Deductible Loss on Disposal of Fixed Assets | Tax loss = written down tax value − sale proceeds. Include scrapping losses. Exclude depreciation recovered |
| 60 | Investment Boost Asset Value | Total value of assets claimed for Investment Boost (exclude private use portion). Cannot be negative |

## KEY FORMULAS

```
Gross Profit (Box 6) = Sales (Box 2) − COGS, where COGS = Opening Stock (Box 3) + Purchases (Box 4) − Closing Stock (Box 5)
  i.e. Box 6 = Box 2 − Box 3 − Box 4 + Box 5

Total Income (Box 11) = Box 6 + Box 7 + Box 8 + Box 9 + Box 10

Total Expenses (Box 25) = Box 12 + Box 13 + ... + Box 24

Net Profit Before Tax (Box 27) = Box 11 − Box 25 ± Box 26

Taxable Profit (Box 29) = Box 27 ± Box 28

Total Assets (Box 43) = Box 30 + Box 31 + ... + Box 42

Total Current Liabilities (Box 48) = Box 44 + Box 45 + Box 46 + Box 47

Total Liabilities (Box 50) = Box 48 + Box 49

Owners' Equity (Box 51) = Box 43 − Box 50
```

---

## VALIDATION CHECKS
1. Box 43 (Total Assets) = Box 50 (Total Liabilities) + Box 51 (Owners' Equity)
2. Box 11 = sum of Boxes 6–10
3. Box 25 = sum of Boxes 12–24
4. Box 27 = Box 11 − Box 25 ± Box 26
5. Box 29 = Box 27 ± Box 28
6. Box 48 = sum of Boxes 44–47
7. Box 50 = Box 48 + Box 49
8. Opening stock (Box 3) should match prior year closing stock
9. Figures should be GST-exclusive unless accounts are GST-inclusive
